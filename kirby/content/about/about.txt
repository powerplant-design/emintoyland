Sexologist, certified holistic sex educator, researcher, writer, podcaster, host and sex toy expert. I provide fun, science-backed sex education to people of all sexualities and genders.

I'm kink-aware, queer-friendly and a total nerd when it comes to sex. If there is a book, film, tv show or podcast about sex or pleasure, I'm probably into it. Because I love to study, learn about and talk about sex.
