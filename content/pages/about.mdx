## I'm Emma Hewitt-Johnson (CHSE)

Sexologist, certified holistic sex educator, researcher, writer, podcaster, host & sex toy expert. I provide fun, science-backed sex education to people of all sexualities & genders.

I'm kink-aware, queer-friendly and a total nerd when it comes to sex. If there's a book, film, tv show or podcast about sex or pleasure, I'm probably into it. Because I love to study, learn about and talk about sex.

My career in sex education started many moons ago when I was hired as a content writing intern at Adulttoymegastore in 2014. At the time, I had never even tried a sex toy, now I have more toys than I can fit in my house and I'm a leading New Zealand sex toy expert.

I also create video content for Adulttoymegastore and write & co-host the award-winning ATMS podcast the Electric Rodeo with my pal Tash Bull.

I've worked with [Gender Minorities Aotearoa](https://genderminorities.com/), the Capital & Coast District Health Board, Tend, Women's Health Week & [AWWA](https://awwaperiodcare.com/).
